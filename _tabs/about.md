# About

Hi, I am `wh0islu`.

This blog is where I keep writeups, notes, and technical logs about the things I
am studying or building.

Most posts here are focused on:

- web security;
- CTFs and lab writeups;
- ML/AI experiments;
- programming notes;
- Linux, tools, and debugging.

I like writing in a practical style: what happened, what I tried, what worked,
and what I learned from it.

# Why This Blog Exists

Some details are easy to forget after solving a problem once.

This site is my public notebook for those details. If a command, bug, idea,
payload, model test, or configuration saves time later, it belongs here.
